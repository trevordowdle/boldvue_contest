How many videos can I post?

You can post as many as you like. In fact, the more videos you make, the greater the chance you'll get selected.

 

Which videos will be judged?

The videos in the top 20% of participants (counted by the total number of likes for that video) will be automatically selected to enter the next round of judging.

 

How will my videos be judged?

The judges will critique your videos based on four areas:
  1. Sincerity (Not acting)–Answer the question like you really want the job
  2. Conciseness–A good interview answer takes between 1-3 minutes
  3. Professionalism–Dressed to impress and using proper language that isn't casual or sloppy
  4. Content–Use concrete and detailed examples to prove you're fit for the job

 

How do you pick the winner?

After the deadline, the top 20% most-liked videos with #boldvuedreamjob will enroll in a selection round where the Boldvue team will select ten videos they feel meet or exceed the criteria. Then, another team of HR and other hiring professionals will view the top ten videos and select the winner.

 

ANY company? I don't believe it.

You better believe it. We are confident that we'll be able to use your video to win over any HR rep at any company. And if we can’t set up an interview with our vast number of connections, we’ll give you $300 to go and get a hiring coach (or you can pocket the money, that works too).

 

When does the contest end?

April 14 at midnight, and the winners will be contacted the following week.

 

Can I share the same video on social media more than once?

Yes! This is not a loophole–if you want your same friends to keep liking new posts of the same video over and over, you can.

 

Where can I share my videos?

Facebook and Twitter. Any others mean nothing to us.
